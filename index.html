Jimpardy

Director: AlysshaRose Jordan-Bird

When a humiliating workout video of a jaded former child star goes viral, he retaliates by forcing a jock, an influencer, and his therapist to play a twisted game of Jeopardy.

Dark Comedy | 7 Min | English

Popular on Short List

Punk Rock Grrrly Pop

Director: Eliza Hardin

Punk Rock Grrrly Pop offers an intimate look at the punk band the Grrrly Pops as they navigate their first major tour.

From the exhilaration of sold-out shows to sleepless nights in a cramped tour bus, the film captures the raw reality of a band adjusting to fame.

Documentary | 45 Min | English

Hipster Wonderland

Director: Stephanie Kara Jewell

It's the summer of 2011, and an eclectic group of strangers bonds on a quest through Brooklyn for the mythical underground club the Castle of Wonders.

As the night goes on, their journey becomes an absurd, psychedelic odyssey that feels more like Wonderland than New York City.

Fantasy | 15 Min | English

Bots! Bots! Bots!

Director: Libby Brown

Humans and Humanoids live in harmony in Echo Springs, a utopia created by OmniSmile Industries, until the town's humanoid mayor is brutally dismantled.

Detective Stella DeVaughn and her humanoid partner, Andria Roid, have until sundown to solve the crime and stop the humanoids from control-alt-deleting humanity in revenge.

Sci-Fi | 30 Mins | English
